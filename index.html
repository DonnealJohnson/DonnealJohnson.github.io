About Me

I'm a frontend developer with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, along with experience using Bootstrap to create responsive, user-friendly designs. My passion lies in crafting clean, functional web interfaces that enhance user experiences. My journey into web development began during my time as a logistics supervisor in the U.S. Army, where I developed problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and a commitment to delivering results. Now, I’m channeling those strengths into building engaging websites and applications. Currently, I’m expanding my skills to become a full-stack developer, diving into backend technologies to create seamless, end-to-end web solutions. My goal is to become a well-rounded full-stack developer and contribute to projects that make a meaningful impact. When I’m not coding, I enjoy watching movies, reading my bible, and spending time with my family.
